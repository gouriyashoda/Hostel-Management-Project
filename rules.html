Hostel Rules

Rules Are Made for you

  • Student must not take law into their own hands, but must report all disputes to the hostel warden.

  • Student shall not remain absent from their hostels during their night without prier permission of the warden.

  • Every student residing in the hostel must join the mess attached to the hostel,individual cooking is not permipted,they are not allowed to cooke anything in their rooms.
